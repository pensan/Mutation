It's the year 1963, in a secret shady lab in the vicinity of Hallein,
there has happend the inexplicable:
The bacteria have evolved and they are trying to escape!

The bacteria utilize neural networks with genetic algorithms (Neuro-evolution) in order to autonomously learn how to progress within the level.

This project has evolved during the GameJam at the Schmiede Festival 2016.
